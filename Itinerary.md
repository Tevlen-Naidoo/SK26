  [Google](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=National+Folk+Museum+of+Korea) · [Kakao](https://map.kakao.com/?q=국립민속박물관)

  [Google](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Insadong+Seoul) · [Kakao](https://map.kakao.com/?q=인사동)

*Why Tuesday: War Memorial & Leeum are closed Mondays. National Museum of Korea, War Memorial and Leeum are all within ~2 km of each other in Yongsan/Hannam — no cross-city travel.*

- **09:30–13:00** — **National Museum of Korea** (Yongsan). Korea's flagship museum — it's huge, give it 3 hrs. (**Free**; Tue 09:30–17:30, last entry 17:00. The reflective pond + pagoda are great photos.) Lunch at the museum café or nearby.
  [Google](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=National+Museum+of+Korea) · [Kakao](https://map.kakao.com/?q=국립중앙박물관)

  [Google](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=War+Memorial+of+Korea) · [Kakao](https://map.kakao.com/?q=전쟁기념관)
- **16:30–18:00** — *(Optional, if energy holds)* **Leeum Museum of Art** (Hannam, ~2 km). Samsung's superb art museum — traditional + contemporary. (Tue–Sun 10:00–18:00, **closed Mon**; permanent collection free with reservation, special shows ~₩20,000.)
  [Google](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Leeum+Museum+of+Art) · [Kakao](https://map.kakao.com/?q=리움미술관)

  - Itaewon: [Google](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Itaewon+Seoul) · [Kakao](https://map.kakao.com/?q=이태원)

## 💡 Practical tips
- **Transit cards:** Buy a **T-money card** (works on all Seoul/Busan metro + buses + many taxis). Top up at any convenience store.
- **Apps:** Kakao Map + Naver Map (navigation), Papago (translation), KakaoT (taxis), Klook/Trip.com (discount attraction tickets & KTX).
- **Money:** Card is widely accepted; keep some cash for markets & Jeju.
- **Weather:** Late July is hot, humid and is **monsoon/typhoon season** — pack a light rain jacket & umbrella; outdoor shows (Banpo fountain, any drone shows) can be cancelled for weather.
- **Pre-book:** Everland tickets, Haeundae Sky Capsule, KTX (1 Aug), Jeju rental car, and check the Lotte Giants schedule.
- **Dropped from original:** Museum San (Wonju, 140 km — too far); the Gwangalli drone show (Sat-only, clashes with departure).
